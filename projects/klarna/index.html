Who am I?

I wish to tell you in person! Then you could ask more about how you would benefit from my years as an event organizer; having planned and executed festivals for 5000 people. You could also ask about how I managed 700 volunteers to get the job done, for example without money as an incentive. I try to continuously expand my comfort zone, trying new things in new ways: like evaluating crazy business ideas through participation in start-up events. My goal is to continue to improve as an enabler: enhancing team performances, reduce all kind of waste (result, time, money) and become an expert in making everything easier and faster for other people (both internal and external!).

Quality is not an act, it is a habit

My background? A student at KTH – Royal Institute of technology that strive to utilize the synergies between the International Master’s programme in Project Management and Operational Development, with focus on quantitative statistical analysis and management systems, and the Bachelor Programme in Mechanical Engineering, with focus on quality management and process design. Seizing the opportunity – Continuously improve
